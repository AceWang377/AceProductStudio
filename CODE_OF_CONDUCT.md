# Code of Conduct

AceProductStudio follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, practical, and generous with context.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming language.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about risks and tradeoffs.
- Keep review comments focused on the code, docs, design, or behavior.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Avoid sharing private data, credentials, or customer information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests away from the project.
- Pressuring maintainers to disclose secrets, customer data, or private operational details.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject pull requests; or block participants when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
